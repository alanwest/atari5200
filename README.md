# Atari 5200

Information and photographs for an Atari 5200 in need of repairs.

## Information

Below are answers to questions found on the [Best Electronics website](https://www.best-electronics-ca.com/ordering.htm).

> What is the model number of the Atari product? (of the 20 to 30 different Atari models)

5200

> What are the exact Symptoms you are getting?

Video and audio does not work

> Has this problem all of sudden happened, it has been getting worse or it was working fine before?

I have taken this Atari out of storage. It has been in my family since it was new.

> What were you doing when the problem started?

Left it in storage for many years. Last time I recall it was functional was the early 2000s.

> Do you have and are using the Atari made Stock Power supply for the product?

Yes the power supply is the original one that came with the unit.

> If yes, what is the Atari Stock Power supply part number.

CO18187. See photo below.

> What have you done to the Atari product so far?

I have only attempted to power it on.

> Has anybody else been inside the Atari product that you know of?

No.
